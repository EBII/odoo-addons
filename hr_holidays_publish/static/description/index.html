HR Holiydays Publish

Share information with your workmates

Let your workmates know when you won't be at the office, thus contributing to a better organisation of the company.

No need to go through the HR people (or your boss!) in order to adapt your requests to your workmates' scheduled leaves.

Control who can see what

It's up to you to define who can see your leaves.

You can decide to tell the whole world or just some specific workmates. But if you want to share information only with the people from the same department, that's fine, too.

Examples:

  • tell your best workmate about any approved request of the "sick leave" type.
  • tell your department about any "holiday" request you've made (approved or not).

Easyness

Others will see your leaves just the same way as they see theirs.
